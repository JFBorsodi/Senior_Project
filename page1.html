Weekly Update

Week One (1/22-1/26)

During week one our team met on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 11:00-1:00 each day. The meetings started with discussing changes to the way robots are generated. After testing out a few robots using the new generation method, we decided that the terrain generation should be adjusted accordingly. The team decided it would be best to change the robot and terrain generation method. The changes made will make the project more realistic in terms of evolution. As the week progressed,we discussed the creation and layout of our website and the first presentation taking place on Monday (1/29).
