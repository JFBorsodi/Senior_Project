Week Ten Update Photos

  • Joints!

After some more testing one of our biggest concerns was the users ability to know exactly how the bot was connected together. Rendering hundreds of joints throughout the simulation turned out to be very resource heavy and would limit the softwares ability to run smoothly. The new joints were designed purely as a visual aid with no effect on the bots fitness and minimal impact on the softwares performance. To accomplish this we only render these joints on the current bot in view, the previous generations winner. These joints are also excluded from the the physics the other blocks must deal with to help with performance.
